How It Works

New Organizer? Learn the basics and get a quick overview on how ChalChaliye's platform will save you time, money and your sanity!!
Written by ChalChaliye

1. Create a Beautiful Booking Page

In just a few minutes, create a beautiful, shareable booking page with itineraries, photos and custom details all in one place.

2. Collect Payments

Share the booking page with participants who book and pay directly on ChalChaliye. 

3. Manage Bookings & Participants 

Our organizer dashboard allows you to easily manage your trips and communications with your participants.

Who Uses ChalChaliye 

ChalChaliye is used by travel companies, individual organizers as well as travellers. Our users include large travel companies, tour operators and DMCs (destination management companies), but also smaller players such as individual travel agents or student trip organizers, students groups looking to go on a trip, couples searching for safe travels, and many more.. 

FIT (Free Independent Traveler) arrangements, custom group trips, scheduled departure trips, MBA student treks, are all being organized through our site.

Travel organizers hate wasting countless hours and bugging people for money. They use ChalChaliye to spend more time doing what they do best - organizing fantastic trips.

How much does it cost to use ChalChaliye?

Beta version of ChalChaliye is absolutely free to use.
